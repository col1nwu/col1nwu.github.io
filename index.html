About Me

My name is Daizong Wu, and you can call me Colin if you want. I come from Hangzhou, the capital of Zhejiang province. I'm currently a graduate student studying Computer Science at USC Viterbi School of Engineering.

Before I came to USC, I was a Software Engineer Intern at NetEase Youdao from Sept 2021 to Dec 2021. At there, I was mainly in charge of developing and maintaining back-end applications. I gained abundant hands-on experience with Spring Boot and MySQL database. Besides, I also conducted Big-Data analysis using Hive and Spark for marketing department for one month there.

I graduated from Purdue University with Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science & Agricultural Engineering. My undergrad program was jointly offered by Purdue University and China Agricultural University, hence I was given the opportunity to study at Purdue for two years. I discovered the power of Computer Science at Purdue and decided to take it as my second major.

As you can see, I mainly code in Java and have many experiences with the Spring Boot framework to build back-end applications, but I'm also seeking other ways to develop web applications, such as React and Django.
