About Me

Hi everybody! My name is Daizong Wu. Call me Colin if you want. I come from Hangzhou, the capital of Zhejiang province. It's a beautiful and viable city on the east coast of China.

I spent my first 15 years in my hometown and then went to Beijing to study in senior high school. I'm grateful about my time in Beijing because I got the chance to experience lives in big city and finally realized what I should be pursuing in my life.

Well, my undergraduate career is a bit complicated... Firstly, I was enrolled in a "2+2" program in Agricultural Engineering jointly offered by China Agricultural Univeristy and Purdue University. After finishing my first two-year study at CAU, I eventually booked a ticket to West Lafayette and continued my undergrad career at Purdue University. At the beginning of my second year at Purdue, I was admitted to take Computer Science as a second major, and I took the offer without a hesitation.

I'm currently a graduate student studying Computer Science at USC Viterbi School of Engineering. I love the California sunshine!

My best programming skill is "Ctrl C+V". You can refer me as a "CV Programmer" too -- "CV" does NOT mean "Computer Vision" here! I play basketball when I'm free, and my favorite basketball player is LeBron James.
